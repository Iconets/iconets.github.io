About Me

I am Christian Onyeoziri, Head of the Vocational and Technical Department at Innov8 Hub , a leading innovation and technology development center. With over a decade of experience in manufacturing, fabrication, and production systems, I specialize in CNC machining, CAM programming, precision fabrication, and industrial automation.

My core expertise lies in bridging the gap between conceptual design and production-ready systems. I focus on transforming functional prototypes into scalable, manufacturable products through rigorous engineering analysis, process optimization, and sound production planning practices.

In my role, I oversee the design and delivery of comprehensive hands-on technical training programs while coordinating fabrication initiatives that integrate engineering fundamentals with industrial best practices. I am responsible for ensuring that production workflows are optimized for efficiency, reliability, and scalability across diverse manufacturing environments.

My approach to engineering is rooted in the belief that technology must address tangible real-world challenges while simultaneously building sustainable technical capacity within organizations and communities. I place strong emphasis on practical problem-solving, system reliability, and long-term manufacturability.

Throughout my career, I have contributed to the development of over 250 functional prototypes and eight Minimum Viable Products (MVPs), supporting the full product lifecycle from innovation and proof-of-concept through to commercialization and manufacturing deployment. My work consistently emphasizes process rigor, technical excellence, and the practical application of advanced manufacturing technologies.
