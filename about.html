About Me

I am Christian Onyeoziri, Head of the Vocational and Technical Department at Innov8 Hub , a leading innovation and technology development center. With over a decade of experience in manufacturing, fabrication, and production systems, I specialize in CNC machining, CAM programming, precision fabrication, and industrial automation.

My core expertise lies in bridging the gap between conceptual design and production-ready systems. I focus on transforming functional prototypes into scalable, manufacturable products through rigorous engineering analysis, process optimization, and sound production planning practices.
